# Domain Research

## General Project Notes

* Findings are not diagnoses.
* Explanations should remain educational and neutral.
* Medical terms should trigger glossary generation.
* Findings should generate doctor discussion questions.
* The AI should avoid treatment recommendations.

---

## Chest X-Ray Reports

### Cardiomegaly

**Meaning:**
Enlargement of the heart seen on a Chest X-Ray.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Shortness of breath
* Fatigue
* Swelling in legs (may occur in some patients)

**Common Causes:**

* High blood pressure
* Heart valve disorders
* Heart failure

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Mild cardiomegaly noted."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report suggests that the heart appears slightly larger than normal. This finding may be associated with several heart-related conditions and should be discussed with a healthcare professional."

**Project Notes:**

* Cardiomegaly is a finding, not a diagnosis.
* The AI should avoid suggesting specific diseases.
* The explanation should remain neutral and educational.

---

### Pleural Effusion

**Meaning:**
Accumulation of excess fluid in the pleural space (the area between the lungs and the chest wall).

**Common Symptoms:**

* Shortness of breath
* Chest pain
* Dry cough

**Common Causes:**

* Heart failure
* Pneumonia
* Lung infections
* Kidney disease
* Liver disease

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Small left pleural effusion noted."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report indicates a buildup of fluid around the lung. This finding can occur due to various medical conditions and should be discussed with your doctor for proper evaluation."

**Project Notes:**

* Pleural effusion is a finding, not a diagnosis.
* The AI should avoid identifying a specific cause.
* The explanation should remain neutral and educational.

---

### Pneumonia

**Meaning:**
An infection that causes inflammation of the air sacs in one or both lungs.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Cough
* Fever
* Chest pain
* Shortness of breath

**Common Causes:**

* Bacterial infection
* Viral infection
* Fungal infection

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Right lower lobe consolidation consistent with pneumonia."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report suggests an infection in the lung that may be causing inflammation. Your doctor will consider your symptoms and other tests when determining the appropriate treatment."

**Project Notes:**

* Pneumonia may appear as consolidation on a Chest X-Ray.
* The AI should explain the finding without recommending medications.
* The explanation should remain educational.

---

### Pneumothorax

**Meaning:**
A condition where air collects between the lung and chest wall, causing partial or complete lung collapse.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Sudden chest pain
* Shortness of breath
* Rapid breathing

**Common Causes:**

* Chest injury
* Lung disease
* Medical procedures
* Spontaneous occurrence

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Small right-sided pneumothorax identified."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report indicates the presence of air outside the lung, which may cause the lung to partially collapse. This finding should be reviewed by a healthcare professional."

**Project Notes:**

* Pneumothorax is an important radiological finding.
* The AI should avoid discussing severity unless explicitly stated in the report.
* The explanation should encourage medical follow-up.

## Bone X-Ray Reports

### Fracture

**Meaning:**
A break or crack in a bone.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Pain
* Swelling
* Bruising
* Difficulty moving the affected area

**Common Causes:**

* Falls
* Accidents
* Sports injuries
* Osteoporosis

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Fracture of the distal radius identified."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report shows a break in one of the bones. The severity and treatment depend on the type and location of the fracture."

**Project Notes:**

* Fracture is a finding, not a treatment recommendation.
* The AI should explain the affected bone if mentioned.
* The system should avoid suggesting treatment plans.

---

### Osteoarthritis

**Meaning:**
A degenerative joint condition caused by gradual wear and tear of joint cartilage.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Joint pain
* Stiffness
* Reduced range of motion
* Swelling

**Common Causes:**

* Aging
* Joint overuse
* Previous injuries
* Obesity

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Mild osteoarthritic changes seen in the knee joint."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report suggests wear-and-tear changes in the joint. These changes are common with aging and may contribute to joint pain or stiffness."

**Project Notes:**

* Osteoarthritis is commonly seen in Bone X-Ray reports.
* Severity terms such as mild, moderate, or severe may appear.
* The AI should explain findings without recommending medications or treatments.

---

### Dislocation

**Meaning:**
A condition in which the bones of a joint are forced out of their normal position.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Severe pain
* Swelling
* Visible deformity
* Limited movement

**Common Causes:**

* Falls
* Sports injuries
* Trauma
* Accidents

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Anterior shoulder dislocation noted."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report indicates that a bone within the joint has moved out of its normal position. The affected joint and severity should be discussed with your healthcare provider."

**Project Notes:**

* Commonly seen in Bone X-Ray reports.
* The AI should explain which joint is involved if mentioned.
* The system should avoid suggesting treatment methods.

---

### Joint Space Narrowing

**Meaning:**
A reduction in the space between the bones of a joint, often indicating cartilage loss.

**Common Symptoms:**

* Joint pain
* Stiffness
* Reduced flexibility
* Difficulty with movement

**Common Causes:**

* Osteoarthritis
* Joint degeneration
* Previous joint injuries

**Example Report Phrase:**
"Medial compartment joint space narrowing observed."

**Patient-Friendly Explanation:**
"The report suggests that the space within the joint is smaller than usual. This finding is often associated with wear-and-tear changes in the joint."

**Project Notes:**

* Frequently associated with osteoarthritis.
* Severity may be described as mild, moderate, or severe.
* The AI should explain the finding without recommending treatment.

## Common Medical Terms

### Finding

**Meaning:**
An observation or abnormality identified in a medical image or report.

### Impression

**Meaning:**
The radiologist's summary of the most important findings and overall interpretation.

### Degenerative Changes

**Meaning:**
Gradual wear-and-tear changes affecting bones, joints, or tissues over time.

### Mild

**Meaning:**
A small or early-stage abnormality with limited severity.

### Moderate

**Meaning:**
An abnormality of intermediate severity.

### Severe

**Meaning:**
A significant abnormality that may require prompt medical attention.
