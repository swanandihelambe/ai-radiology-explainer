# Problem Statement

Chest and Bone X-Ray reports are written primarily for healthcare professionals and often contain complex medical terminology that can be difficult for patients to understand.

As a result, many patients experience confusion, anxiety, and misinterpretation of their medical findings. They frequently rely on internet searches, which may provide incomplete, inaccurate, or misleading information.

The goal of this project is to develop an AI-assisted platform that converts Chest and Bone X-Ray reports into patient-friendly explanations while preserving the original meaning of the report.

The platform aims to improve patient understanding, health literacy, and doctor-patient communication without providing diagnoses or treatment recommendations.

## Objectives

* Simplify complex X-Ray report terminology
* Improve patient understanding of reports
* Generate easy-to-read explanations
* Provide definitions for medical terms
* Suggest questions patients can discuss with their doctors
* Maintain transparency and medical responsibility

## Target Users

* Patients receiving Chest or Bone X-Ray reports
* Caregivers
* Medical students (secondary use)
* Non-medical users interested in understanding radiology reports

## Scope (Version 1)

Supported Report Types:

* Chest X-Ray Reports
* Bone X-Ray Reports

Out of Scope:

* CT Reports
* MRI Reports
* Ultrasound Reports
* Direct X-Ray Image Analysis
* Disease Diagnosis
* Treatment Recommendations
