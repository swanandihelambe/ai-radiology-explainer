Report 4
Examination: X-Ray Left Shoulder, AP Internal and External Rotation Views
Findings: The humeral head is displaced anteriorly and inferiorly relative to the glenoid fossa, consistent with anterior glenohumeral dislocation. A compression deformity is noted at the posterolateral humeral head, consistent with a Hill-Sachs lesion. No associated greater tuberosity fracture identified. Glenoid margin appears intact.
Impression: Anterior dislocation of the left glenohumeral joint with Hill-Sachs deformity. No bony Bankart lesion identified. Urgent reduction recommended.
