Report 3
Examination: X-Ray Right Hip, AP and Frog-Leg Lateral Views
Findings: Moderate to severe joint space narrowing is noted at the right hip, predominantly superolaterally. Subchondral sclerosis and osteophyte formation are present at the acetabular rim and femoral head. Subchondral cyst formation is observed at the superior femoral head. No acute fracture or dislocation. Femoral neck appears intact.
Impression: Moderate-to-severe osteoarthritis of the right hip joint. No acute fracture.
