Report 10
Examination: X-Ray Right Shoulder, AP and Scapular Y Views
Findings: Moderate joint space narrowing is present at the glenohumeral articulation. Subchondral sclerosis and marginal osteophyte formation are noted at the inferior humeral head and glenoid. Mild superior migration of the humeral head is observed, suggestive of rotator cuff insufficiency. No acute fracture or dislocation. Acromioclavicular joint demonstrates mild degenerative changes.
Impression: Moderate glenohumeral osteoarthritis with degenerative changes. Superior humeral head migration suggesting chronic rotator cuff pathology. Clinical correlation recommended.
