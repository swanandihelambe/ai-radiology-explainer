Report 2
Examination: X-Ray Left Wrist, PA, Lateral, and Oblique Views
Findings: A transverse fracture is identified at the distal radius with dorsal angulation of approximately 15 degrees and mild comminution at the fracture site. The ulnar styloid appears intact. Radiocarpal and distal radioulnar joint spaces are maintained. No carpal bone fracture identified.
Impression: Distal radius fracture (Colles-type) with dorsal angulation. No associated ulnar styloid fracture. Clinical correlation and orthopedic consultation recommended.
