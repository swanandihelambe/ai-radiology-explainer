Report 8
Examination: X-Ray Left Hip, AP Pelvis and Cross-Table Lateral Views
Findings: An intertrochanteric fracture of the left proximal femur is identified, demonstrating varus angulation and moderate displacement. The fracture line extends from the greater to lesser trochanter. No subtrochanteric extension identified. The femoral neck is intact. Acetabulum appears preserved. Contralateral hip shows no acute pathology.
Impression: Displaced left intertrochanteric femoral fracture. Urgent orthopedic surgical evaluation recommended.
